Becoming Embedded Freelancer

Unlock multiple streams of income as an embedded engineer, by becoming an embedded freelancer.

Learn how to earn more money (even more than your main job) working on freelance projects using a proven path explained from an embedded engineer's point-of-view.

"I want to be a freelancer working on embedded projects, but I don't know how."

Hi! I'm Rosmianto, an embedded systems engineer who used to be stuck with a fragile single stream of income.

Let me first ask you a question: what is the most generic way to make more money as an embedded engineer? Two: promotion or switching company.

But promotion isn't straighforward, you can't work twice as hard and expect to be promoted next year. That's not how it works. Promotion is also subject to execs' approval, subject to company financial and political situation. Hell, it's even subject to competition from your peers (they also want to be promoted right?).

Even when you get promoted, how much salary hike you'll get?

Not even 10% (mine was worse at 5%). That's excluding yearly inflation (around 3% in my country).

All that for a inflated title and more pressure and stress at work? No thanks.

Fine, you might be tempted to switch company. Now, the question is: can you find a significantly better company than yours currently? And can you actually pass their bar?

Why significantly better? Because, trust me, in many cases, switching company is just trading off one thing with another. Take my case for example, I was working at this company: paid good enough, work from home, close from my house, good friends, no politics.

If I want to make more money by switching company, that means I have to find another company with the good things above, plus with better pay. And let me tell you, it's hard. And not straightforward. Most times I need to sacrifice 3 things just to get 1 thing (better pay).

Also, no thanks.

I have tried promotion and switching stuff, and honestly those was only my wants, not my needs. What I truly need is just some income boosts.

Enter: freelancing world.

It's like getting paid by multiple bosses.

 

I didn't come from money, I had to support myself in school, I have I crave for financial freedom

In this book, I want to guide you on how to be a freelancer as an embedded engineer. I will share with you my proven method of doing freelance. I will also share with you my framework you can steal.

In other words, I designed this book precisely to be to-the-point, zero bullshit, meat-only content, so you can extract my knowledge in this book as fast as possible. And thus, make money as a freelancer as soon as possible.
